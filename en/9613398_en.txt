'''Piechowice''' {{IPAr|pl|p|j|e|h|o|'|w|i|c|e}} ({{lang-de|Petersdorf}}) is a town in [[Jelenia Góra County]], [[Lower Silesian Voivodeship]], in south-western [[Poland]]. Prior to 1945 it was in [[Germany]].

It lies approximately {{convert|10|km|mi|0}} south-west of [[Jelenia Góra]], and {{convert|104|km|mi|0}} west of the regional capital [[Wrocław]].

As of 2006, the town has a population of 6,494.
