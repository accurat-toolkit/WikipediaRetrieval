'''Żarów''' {{IPAr|pl|'|ż|a|r|u|f}} ({{lang-de|Saarau}}) is a town in [[Świdnica County]], [[Lower Silesian Voivodeship]], in south-western [[Poland]]. It is the seat of the administrative district ([[gmina]]) called [[Gmina Żarów]]. Prior to 1945 it was in [[Germany]]. (For more information about the history of the region, see [[Silesia]].)

The town lies approximately {{convert|11|km|mi|0}} north-east of [[Świdnica]], and {{convert|45|km|mi|0}} south-west of the regional capital [[Wrocław]].

As at 2006, the town has a population of 6,902.
