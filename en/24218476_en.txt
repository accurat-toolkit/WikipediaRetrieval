'''Iecava''' is a [[river]] in [[Latvia]]. Iecava's [[Source (river or stream)|source]] is formed from several [[Spring (hydrosphere)|springs]] near [[Daudzese]] [[village]], [[Jaunjelgava municipality]]. The river's mouth located 4 km below [[Jelgava]] [[city]] in [[Lielupe]] river.<ref>[http://www.jrpic.lv/picdown/projekti/hidro/3.1.2.pdf Lielāko upju un to baseinu izvietojums] (The location of the biggest rivers and their basins in Latvia, in Latvian)</ref>

Iecava river is flowing through [[Taurkalne low land|Taurkalne]] and [[Upmale low land]] where it has mainly [[Forest|wooded]] [[coast]]s.<ref>[http://www.jrpic.lv/picdown/projekti/hidro/3.1.2.pdf Lielāko upju un to baseinu izvietojums] (The location of the biggest rivers and their basins in Latvia, in Latvian)</ref> Near [[Iecava]] [[town]] it crossing [[Zemgale low land]] and in [[Tīreļi low land]] it reaches Lielupe river.

The main water source (more than 50%) for Iecava river is water from melting [[snow]], but water from [[groundwater]]s is minimal (4-5%); because of that river usually overflows in [[Spring (season)|springs]] and has low water level in [[summer]]s.<ref>[http://www.jrpic.lv/picdown/projekti/hidro/3.1.2.pdf Lielāko upju un to baseinu izvietojums] (The location of the biggest rivers and their basins in Latvia, in Latvian)</ref>
