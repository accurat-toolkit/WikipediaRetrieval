'''''Ranunculus flammula''''' L. ('''Lesser Spearwort''') is a species of [[perennial plant|perennial]] [[herbaceous]] plants in the genus ''[[Ranunculus]]'' (buttercup), growing in damp places throughout the [[Boreal Kingdom]]. It is very closely related to ''[[Ranunculus reptans|R. reptans]]'' L., which is distinguished by prostrate and more slender stems, narrower leaves and smaller flowers and is sometimes included within ''R. flammula'' sensu lato as a variety (''R. flammula'' var. ''reptans'' (L.) E. Meyer).  It flowers June/July.
