'''Rucava municipality''' ({{lang-lv|Rucavas novads}}) is a [[municipality]] in [[Courland]], [[Latvia]]. The municipality was formed in [[2009]] by [[Merger (politics)|merging]] [[Dunika parish]] and [[Rucava parish]] the administrative centre being [[Rucava]].
