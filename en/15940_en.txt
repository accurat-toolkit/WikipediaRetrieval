==Events==
* [[565]] &ndash; [[Patriarch Eutychius of Constantinople|Eutychius]] is deposed as [[Patriarch of Constantinople]] by [[John Scholasticus]].
*[[1506]] &ndash; The first contingent of 150 [[Swiss Guard]]s arrives at the [[Vatican City|Vatican]].
*[[1521]] &ndash; [[Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor]], opens the [[Diet of Worms]].
*[[1771]] &ndash; [[Spain]] cedes [[Port Egmont]] in the [[Falkland Islands]] to [[England]].
*[[1824]] &ndash; [[The Ashanti|Ashantis]] defeat [[United Kingdom|British]] forces in the [[Gold Coast (British colony)|Gold Coast]].
*[[1863]] &ndash; The [[January Uprising]] breaks out in [[Poland]], [[Lithuania]] and [[Belarus]]. The aim of the national movement is to regain [[Polish-Lithuanian Commonwealth|Polish-Lithuanian-Ruthenian Commonwealth]] from occupation by [[Russia]].
*[[1877]] &ndash; [[Arthur Tooth]], an [[Anglican]] [[clergyman]] is taken into custody after being prosecuted for using [[Ritualism|ritualist]] practices.
*[[1879]] &ndash; [[Anglo-Zulu War]]: [[Battle of Isandlwana]] &ndash; [[Zulu]] troops defeat British troops.
*  1879   &ndash; Anglo-Zulu War: [[Battle of Rorke's Drift]] &ndash; 139 [[British Army|British soldiers]] successfully defend their [[garrison]] against an intense assault by four to five thousand [[Zulu|Zulu warriors]].
*[[1889]] &ndash; [[Columbia Phonograph]] is formed in [[Washington, D.C.]] 
*[[1890]] &ndash; The [[United Mine Workers of America]] is founded in [[Columbus, Ohio]].
*[[1899]] &ndash; Leaders of six [[Australia]]n colonies meet in [[Melbourne]] to discuss confederation.
*[[1901]] &ndash; [[Edward VII of the United Kingdom|Edward VII]] is proclaimed King after the death of his mother, [[Victoria of the United Kingdom|Queen Victoria]]. 
*[[1905]] &ndash; [[Bloody Sunday (1905)|Bloody Sunday]] in [[St. Petersburg, Russia|St. Petersburg]], beginning of the 1905 revolution.
*[[1906]] &ndash; [[SS Valencia]] runs aground on rocks on [[Vancouver Island]], [[British Columbia]], killing more than 130.
*[[1917]] &ndash; [[World War I]]: [[President of the United States|President]] [[Woodrow Wilson]] calls for "peace without victory" in [[Europe]].
*[[1919]] &ndash; [[Act Zluky]] is signed, unifying the [[Ukrainian People's Republic]] and the [[West Ukrainian National Republic]].
*[[1924]] &ndash; [[Ramsay MacDonald]] becomes the first [[Labour Party (UK)|Labour]] [[Prime Minister of the United Kingdom]]. 
*[[1927]] &ndash; First live radio commentary of a [[football (soccer)|football]] match anywhere in the world, between [[Arsenal F.C.]] and [[Sheffield United F.C.|Sheffield United]] at [[Arsenal Stadium|Highbury]].
*[[1931]] &ndash; Sir [[Isaac Isaacs]] is sworn in as the first [[Australia]]n-born [[Governor-General of Australia]].
*[[1941]] &ndash; [[World War II]]: [[British Empire|British]] and [[Commonwealth of Nations|Commonwealth]] troops capture [[Tobruk]] from [[Regio Esercito|Italian forces]] during [[Operation_Compass#Tobruk|Operation Compass]].
*[[1944]] &ndash; World War II: The [[Allies of World War II|Allies]] commence [[Operation Shingle]] which is an assault on [[Anzio]], [[Italy]].
*[[1946]] &ndash; [[Iran]]: [[Qazi Muhammad]] declares the independent people's [[Republic of Mahabad]] at [[Chuwarchira Square]] in the Kurdish city of [[Mahabad]]. He is the new president; [[Hadschi Baba Scheich]] is the [[prime minister]].
*  1946   &ndash; Creation of the ''Central Intelligence Group'', forerunner of the [[Central Intelligence Agency]].
*[[1947]] &ndash; [[KTLA]], the first commercial [[television station]] west of the [[Mississippi River]], begins operation in [[Hollywood, California]].
*[[1952]] &ndash; The first [[Jet airliner]], the [[de Havilland Comet]], enters service for [[BOAC]].
*[[1957]] &ndash; [[Israel]] withdraws from the [[Sinai Peninsula]].
*  1957   &ndash; The [[New York City]] "[[Mad Bomber]]", [[George P. Metesky]], is arrested in [[Waterbury, Connecticut]] and is charged with planting more than 30 bombs.
*[[1959]] &ndash; [[Knox Mine Disaster]]: Water breaches the River Slope Mine near [[Pittston, Pennsylvania|Pittston City]], [[Pennsylvania]] in [[Port Griffith, Pennsylvania|Port Griffith]]; 12 miners are killed.
*[[1962]] &ndash; The [[Organization of American States]] suspends [[Cuba]]'s membership.
*[[1963]] &ndash; The [[Elysée treaty]] of cooperation between [[France]] and [[Germany]] is signed by [[Charles de Gaulle]] and [[Konrad Adenauer]].
*[[1968]] &ndash; [[Apollo 5]] lifts off carrying the first [[Lunar module]] into space.
*[[1973]] &ndash; The [[Supreme Court of the United States]] delivers its decision in [[Roe v. Wade]], legalizing elective [[abortion]] in all fifty states.
*  1973   &ndash; A chartered [[Boeing 707]] explodes in flames upon landing at [[Kano Airport]], [[Nigeria]] killing 176.
*[[1984]] &ndash; The [[Apple Macintosh]], the first consumer computer to popularize the [[computer mouse]] and the [[graphical user interface]], is introduced during Super Bowl XVIII with its famous ''"[[1984 (television commercial)|1984]]"'' [[television commercial]].
*[[1987]] &ndash; [[Pennsylvania]] politician [[R. Budd Dwyer]] shoots and kills himself at a press conference on live national [[television]], leading to debates on boundaries in journalism.
*[[1990]] &ndash; [[Robert Tappan Morris, Jr.]] is convicted of releasing the [[Morris worm|1988 Internet Computer worm]].
*[[1991]] &ndash; [[Gulf War]]: Three [[SCUD]]s and one Patriot missile hit [[Ramat Gan]] in [[Israel]], injuring 96 people. Three elderly people die of heart attacks.
*[[1992]] &ndash; Rebel forces occupy [[Zaire]]'s national radio station in [[Kinshasa]] and broadcast a demand for the government's resignation.
*  1992   &ndash; [[Space Shuttle program]]: [[STS-42]] Mission &ndash; Dr. [[Roberta Bondar]] becomes the first [[Canada|Canadian]] woman in space.
*[[1995]] &ndash; [[Israeli-Palestinian conflict]]: [[Beit Lid massacre]] &ndash; In central [[Israel]], near [[Netanya]], two [[suicide bomber]]s from the [[Gaza Strip]] blow themselves up at a military transit point killing 19 Israelis. 
*[[1999]] &ndash; Australian missionary [[Graham Staines]] and his two sons are burned alive by radical [[Hindus]] while sleeping in their car in Eastern [[India]].
*[[2002]] &ndash; [[Kmart Corp]] becomes the largest retailer in [[United States]] history to file for [[Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection]].
*[[2006]] &ndash; [[Evo Morales]] is inaugurated as President of [[Bolivia]], becoming the country's first indigenous president.
