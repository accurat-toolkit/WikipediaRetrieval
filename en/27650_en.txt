==Events==
*[[1400]] &ndash; [[Owain Glyndŵr]] is declared [[Prince of Wales]] by his followers.
*[[1701]] &ndash; [[James Francis Edward Stuart]], sometimes called the "Old Pretender", becomes the [[Jacobitism|Jacobite claimant]] to the thrones of [[England]] and [[Scotland]].
*[[1776]] &ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: the [[Battle of Harlem Heights]] is fought.
*[[1795]] &ndash; The first occupation by [[United Kingdom]] of [[Cape Colony]], [[South Africa]] with the Battle of Hout Bay, after successive victories at the [[Battle of Muizenberg]] and Wynberg, after William III requested protection against revolutionary France's occupation of the Netherlands.
*[[1810]] &ndash; With the ''[[Grito de Dolores]],'' [[Priest|Father]] [[Miguel Hidalgo]] begins [[Mexican War of Independence|Mexico's fight for independence from Spain]].
*[[1812]] &ndash; [[Russia]]ns set fire to [[Moscow]] shortly after midnight &ndash; the city burns down completely days later.
*[[1863]] &ndash; [[Robert College]] of [[Istanbul]]-[[Turkey]], the first American educational institution outside the United States, is founded by [[Christopher Robert]], an American philanthropist.
*[[1893]] &ndash; Settlers race in [[Oklahoma]] for prime land in the [[Cherokee Outlet|Cherokee Strip]].
*[[1901]] &ndash; [[Alturas]], [[California]], is incorporated as the only city in [[Modoc County]].
*[[1908]] &ndash; [[General Motors Corporation|General Motors]] is founded.
*[[1919]] &ndash; The [[American Legion]] is incorporated.
*[[1920]] &ndash; The [[Wall Street bombing]]: a bomb in a horse wagon explodes in front of the [[J.P.Morgan]] building in [[New York City]] &ndash; 38 are killed and 400 injured.
*[[1931]] &ndash; Hanging of [[Omar Mukhtar]].
*[[1941]] &ndash; [[World War II]]: concerned that [[Reza Shah|Reza Pahlavi]] the [[Pahlavi dynasty|Shah of Persia]] was to align his petroleum-rich country with [[Nazi Germany|Germany]] during [[World War II]], the [[United Kingdom]] and the [[USSR]] [[Anglo-Soviet Invasion of Iran|invade Iran]] in late August and force him to resign in favor of his son, [[Mohammad Reza Pahlavi]].
*[[1945]] &ndash; World War II: Surrender of the Japanese forces in [[Hong Kong]]. The ceremony was presided by British Admiral [[Cecil Harcourt]].
*[[1947]] &ndash; Typhoon Kathleen hit [[Saitama, Saitama|Saitama]], [[Tokyo]] and [[Tone River]] area, at least 1,930 killed.
*[[1955]] &ndash; [[Juan Perón]] is deposed in [[Argentina]].
*[[1963]] &ndash; [[Malaysia]] is formed from [[Federation of Malaya|Malaya]], [[Singapore]], [[British North Borneo]] ([[Sabah]]) and [[Sarawak]].
*[[1966]] &ndash; The [[Metropolitan Opera|Metropolitan Opera House]] opens at [[Lincoln Center]] in [[New York City]] with the world premiere of [[Samuel Barber]]'s [[opera]], ''[[Antony and Cleopatra (opera)|Antony and Cleopatra]]''.
*[[1970]] &ndash; [[King Hussein]] of [[Jordan]] declares military rule following the hijacking of four civilian airliners by the [[Popular Front for the Liberation of Palestine]] (PFLP). This results in the formation of the [[Black September (group)|Black September]] [[Palestinian people|Palestinian]] paramilitary unit.
*[[1975]] &ndash; [[Papua New Guinea]] gains its independence from [[Australia]].
*[[1975]] &ndash; The first prototype of the [[MiG-31]] interceptor makes its maiden flight.
*[[1976]] &ndash; [[Shavarsh Karapetyan]] saves 20 people from the trolleybus that had fallen into [[Erevan]] [[reservoir]].
*[[1978]] &ndash; An earthquake measuring 7.5-7.9 on the [[Richter magnitude scale|Richter scale]] hits the city of [[Tabas]], [[Iran]] killing about 25,000 people.
*[[1982]] &ndash; [[Sabra and Shatila massacre]] in [[Lebanon]].
*[[1987]] &ndash; The [[Montreal Protocol]] is signed to protect the [[ozone layer]] from [[Ozone depletion|depletion]]. 
*[[1991]] &ndash; The trial of deposed [[Panama]]nian dictator [[Manuel Noriega]] begins in the [[United States]].
*[[1992]] &ndash; [[Black Wednesday]]: the [[Pound Sterling]] is forced out of the [[European Exchange Rate Mechanism]] by [[Speculation|currency speculator]]s and is forced to devalue against the [[German mark|Deutschmark]].
*[[2005]] &ndash; [[Camorra]] boss [[Paolo Di Lauro]] is arrested in [[Naples]].
*[[2007]] &ndash; [[One-Two-GO Airlines Flight 269]] carrying 128 crew and passengers crashes in [[Thailand]] killing 89 people.
*[[2009]] &ndash; [[Yukio Hatoyama]] inaugurated as [[Prime Minister of Japan]] after [[Japanese general election, 2009|a landmark election victory]] over [[Taro Aso]].
