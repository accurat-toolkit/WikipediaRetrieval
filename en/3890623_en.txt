'''Neftekamsk''' ({{lang-ru|Нефтека́мск}}) is a city in the [[Bashkortostan|Republic of Bashkortostan]], [[Russia]]. It is located at around {{Coord|56|5|30|N|54|16|0|E |region:RU-BA_type:city}}.

The town covers the territory of around 143 sq.km. It is situated next to the river [[Kama]], 220&nbsp;km away from [[Ufa]]. The town borders on Krasnokamskiy and Yanaulskiy districts of [[Bashkortostan]]. A road network connects the town to [[Ufa]], [[Birsk]], [[Yanaul]], [[Agidel]], [[Oktyabrsky]], [[Izhevsk]], [[Perm]], [[Kazan]]. The nearest railway station is located in [[Amzya]] — a subdivision of the town 15&nbsp;km away. Besides there is [[Neftekamsk Airport]].

The population is 128,957 (2007 est.);<ref name="population"> http://adm.neftekamsk.ru/doc/econom.rar</ref> 122,290 ([[Russian Census (2002)|2002 Census]]) and the town comes the fourth in population in [[Bashkortostan]] after [[Ufa]], [[Sterlitamak]] and [[Salavat, Russia|Salavat]]. An average age of dwellers is 37. People of 49&nbsp;nationalities live here.<ref name="population"/>

Neftekamsk is a large industrial and cultural center of the north-west of [[Bashkortostan]].
