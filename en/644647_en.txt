'''Janów Lubelski''' {{IPAr|pl|'|j|a|n|u|f|-|l|u|'|b|e|l|s|k|i}} is a town in eastern [[Poland]]. It has 11,882 inhabitants (2004).

Situated in the [[Lublin Voivodship]] (since 1999). It is the capital of [[Janów Lubelski County]]. 

It has a large hospital (Samodzielny Publiczny Zespół Zakładów Opieki Zdrowotnej). It also has several tourist attractions - buildings and churches from 17th-18th centuries.

The town was founded in 1640 at the site of an older village.
