'''Kaluga Oblast''' ({{lang-ru|Калу́жская о́бласть}}, ''Kaluzhskaya oblast'') is a [[federal subjects of Russia|federal subject]] of [[Russia]] (an [[oblast]]).  Its administrative center is the city of [[Kaluga]].

==Geography==
===Time zone===
[[File:Map of Russia - Moscow time zone.svg|left|75px]]

Kaluga Oblast is located in the [[Moscow Time|Moscow Time Zone]] (MSK/MSD).  [[Coordinated Universal Time|UTC]] offset is +0300&nbsp;(MSK)/+0400&nbsp;(MSD).
<div style="clear:left"></div>

==History==
It was created in 1944 out of four other oblasts: [[Moskva Oblast]], [[Orel Oblast]], [[Smolensk Oblast]], and [[Tula Oblast]]. It is located at the historical region of the former [[Kaluga Governorate]].
