'''Latvijas Universitātes Stadions''' is a multi-use [[stadium]] in [[Riga]], [[Latvia]].  It is currently used mostly for [[football (soccer)|football]] and [[Rugby football|rugby]] matches and is the home stadium of [[FK Rīga]].  The stadium holds 5,000 people.
